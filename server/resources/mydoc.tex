  \begin{defn*}[definition of the page]
      For $A \subset G$, we set $N_G(A) := \{g \in G | gAg^{-1} = A\}$ and
      $C_G(A) := \{g \in G | gag^{-1} = a, \forall a \in A\}$.
  \end{defn*}
  Note that $C_G(A) \subset N_G(A)$ and $Z(G) = C_G(G)$.
